What is Stamppot?

Arguably this is The Netherlands most famous dish. It consists out of one vegatable and potato mash, served with different toppings to add color and flavour. This is a dish that is very popular during the winter season. As with all traditional dishes, there are many recipes for this dish. So where do you start? Well, this App will get you started!
